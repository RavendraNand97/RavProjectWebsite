About Ravendra Nand

My name is Ravendra Nand, and I am a Data Analyst pursuing a new career opportunity. I graduated from the University of Maryland College Park with a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Science, Data Specialization in December of 2020. In college, I discovered my passion for diving into data, and discovering important facts, information and even stories within the data. I probably did enjoy it before then, in my sciences courses and science projects but did not recognize that I did. You would conduct some experiment and look at the data that was collect and determine a conclusion. Part of the process is making graphs and charts so that readers can better understand the conclusion. Similarly, in data analyst, I would look at data and try to find patterns or information and show the findings through visualizations so people can better understand the data.

As you explore this website, you will find my resume and projects that I worked on or currently in progress. Throughout my time at UMD I took courses that builds up my experience as a data analyst. I have learned many of the tools that is needed to process and analyze data as well as create visualizations for those data. For example, in the Data Visualization course, I crafted various data presentations using different medias, such as Tableau and Power Point. In Data Manipulation course I explored the ways of sourcing, cleaning, and manipulating data to identify patterns or trends in the data. I also performed statistical analysis on data using R in Statistics for Information Science course. I collaborated with a team to clean, analyze, and create visualization for a client organization data set. Their data had poor data integrity, meaning their data was very inconsistent and inaccurate. The product constitutes a clean data set with good data integrity, and a visualization of their data, which helped them better understand how well they did in their yearly events.
